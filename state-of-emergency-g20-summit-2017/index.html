State Of Emergency - G20 Summit 2017

Today was a day full of stress, traffic jam and a lot of negative energy….


https://picload.org/image/rplgigwi/webp.net-resizeimage36.jpg


The G20 summit takes place in my city, Hamburg, as everyone already know. The traffic was totally out of control just one day before the G20 summit should start because Donald Trump landed today and blocked the whole city centre. I was stuck in traffic for three hours and needed to park outside the area and take the train to reach my destination. I really want to mention that the police officer are doing a great job. You see police trucks, weapons and officers everywhere. In addition at least five helicopters are circling around the town to protect the world leaders. 


--------------------------------------------------------------------

I took some pictures out of my car window to show you what happened today.


I literally was staying in that position for over 30 minutes without moving a centimeter! After waiting 50 minutes I was told that the street is blocked you need to drive a different road. If I only knew that before...
