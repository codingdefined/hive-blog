    Are there any fans of Southwestern style food in the house? Anyone? Bueller…..Bueller…….

    ![Southwest Egg Rolls and Avacado Ranch Dipping Sauce.2.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmd5FvatjQNdAM2ZjwB76yKWafuCFh2va2ehzSWo1mvteh/Southwest%20Egg%20Rolls%20and%20Avacado%20Ranch%20Dipping%20Sauce.2.jpg)
    How about avocados? Well, around here avocados are eaten almost religiously and this avocado ranch is such an awesome way to showcase them. It is so good with these egg rolls.
    ![Southwest Egg Rolls and Avacado Ranch Dipping Sauce (6).jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmeAbUqpRNDUAHNX7GGrjLuyAoYfxSikkWcYojLBsC7tz7/Southwest%20Egg%20Rolls%20and%20Avacado%20Ranch%20Dipping%20Sauce%20(6).jpg)
    Okay, so I’m not gonna lie, photographing these egg rolls totally kicked my butt. In fact, I may never try taking pictures of egg rolls again. I’ll add it to my new year’s resolutions or something.
    ![Southwest Egg Rolls and Avacado Ranch Dipping Sauce (3).jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmdhMtKWSmLC3kPjDBGe62J2mUQ8LqfNhXu92GAHtUsBHp/Southwest%20Egg%20Rolls%20and%20Avacado%20Ranch%20Dipping%20Sauce%20(3).jpg)
    Don’t ask me why. I really don’t understand why the most innocent looking food can be so extremely difficult for me sometimes. I just couldn’t capture them the way that I wanted. I won’t even admit how long I spent trying to make them look the way I had planned.
    ![Southwest Egg Rolls and Avacado Ranch Dipping Sauce,.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmeB7TxHNC1XCuqFZzDnJ1dzunvrKySy4maGAXvXP17imE/Southwest%20Egg%20Rolls%20and%20Avacado%20Ranch%20Dipping%20Sauce%2C.jpg)
    Honestly, my best photos are the ones that I take when I am relaxed and not trying to be a perfectionist. I think my mood shows through my photography. If I am having a bad day or just not all that excited about a recipe then it’s a sure bet the pictures are going to suck. For example, my camera had been misbehaving this day and the weather was creating the worst possible lighting which had me doing nothing short of dancing around trying to keep up and capture the right moments. The point is that it just wasn’t happening, I was frustrated, and I came very close to scratching the entire thing.
    ![Southwest Egg Rolls and Avacado Ranch Dipping Sauce (7).jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmcPxgY8zobsk25ibYNUHEtoZ8DhRZiQDdfewZ2rzwdx4B/Southwest%20Egg%20Rolls%20and%20Avacado%20Ranch%20Dipping%20Sauce%20(7).jpg)

I think I’ve said this before and I know that I have told you not to let my amateur photography skills keep you from trying a recipe. I can at least say that these really are delicious. I made them for our Christmas party one year and called them “Feliz Navidad Rolls” or “Santa Fe-egg Rolls”.
    ![Southwest Egg Rolls and Avacado Ranch Dipping Sauce (2).jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmQzuPYwjXzN91CgAYcp6svFrjNCuaKUmnSxas9xkd2Hk7/Southwest%20Egg%20Rolls%20and%20Avacado%20Ranch%20Dipping%20Sauce%20(2).jpg)

I’ve never seen egg roll or wonton wrappers in the stores here so I didn’t have much choice but even if you can buy them I recommend trying them at home at least once. You know, just to say you did.     STEEM-POCALYPSE SURVIVAL GAME SEASON 4 RECAP + GAME OVERVIEW & NEW RULES FOR SEASON 5

    July 10, 2017

    http://i.imgur.com/fS6c6wr.jpg ##

    This has been an incredible adventure!
    --------- Creating, running, and improving the STEEM-Pocalypse Survival Game Contest has been amazing. First of all, I think that it gives some users a great reason to check in here on steemit *every* day. But even more than that, it inspires some users to interact in a way that they normally may not, which not only builds new friendships, but the community as well. Often in this game steemians who were previously strangers get thrown together into scenarios where they must work together in order to survive, and they have a lot of fun doing it. Of course the opportunity to win hundreds of dollars worth of SBD also has its own appeal. https://img1.steemit.com/0x0/http://i.imgur.com/uVNBbyL.jpg ##
    SEASON 4 RECAP
    ---------- Each season has its own special place in my life. For Season 4, it was a great opportunity for the largest participation so far. There is no way that I want to limit or restrict participation, so I will do my best to always keep this contest open to all. Because of that, I am presuming that more and more steemians will participate in each new season, so Season 5 may have *hundreds* of people signing up initially. Since it is an elimination game, most will eventually have to die, but at least whoever wants to join in can at least give themselves the opportunity to win. In Season 4, I implemented a *solo play* to begin with. Individual steemians had to consistently check in on a daily basis to prove their availability and commitment. This was to help streamline the gameplay and make sure that people playing were actually going to be playing the game. I know that situations arise where not everyone can be available each day, but to move the gameplay forward I need participants to be committed to check in and make decisions once every 24 hour period. Another change for Season 4 is that during the beginning solo play, players got the opportunity to collect resources which would come in handy later. Then, when some of them teamed up, they had more to work with. I think that this added an interesting variable to the gameplay and worked well. https://steemitimages.com/0x0/http://i.imgur.com/YUOX7hw.png As will potentially happen with more participants, Season 4 lasted the longest of any season so far too. Since the prize pool increases on a daily basis, this meant the largest prize pool so far too! The winner, @jznsamuel, took home 825 SBD out of the total prize pool of 1100 SBD. It is crazy to think that someone could make so much money just by playing a game for a few minutes online each day, but that is the opportunity that the STEEM-Pocalypse Survival Game Contest can offer now because of steemit! https://img1.steemit.com/0x0/http://i.imgur.com/uVNBbyL.jpg ##
    GAME OVERVIEW
    ---------- For those of you who are new to this game or unfamiliar with how it works. here is a simple overview of the general concept to help you understand. Basically, this game is a "choose your own adventure" style story game. In each round, I come up with different scenarios in which the players must choose an option. Each option has a predetermined outcome, and some will eventually lead to death. As players die, the remaining ones continue playing until the last players die or only one player/team is left alive. Different variable and scenarios come up each round, some of which are based on reality and are more educational and others are just random scenarios that I come up with. By using their knowledge, researching skills, or maybe just guessing the players attempt to pass each round. Situations like avoiding starvation and staying hydrated are mixed in with various perils and dangerous scenarios. I try to keep it fun, yet challenging. Each player has the responsibility to reply to each new post within 24 hours with their decision based on the options given. Simply put, if you are capable of accessing your steemit account, reading my latest STEEM-Pocalypse post, and replying with the number of the option that you are choosing, you will be able to play. https://img1.steemit.com/0x0/http://i.imgur.com/uVNBbyL.jpg ##

    SEASON 5 RULES
    ---------- In many ways, Season 5 will run a lot like Season 4. Individual players will have to make it past the beginning days in order to proceed. Players who do not reply within the 24 hour response period for each round will be eliminated. Players who make it past the initial rounds will be provided with opportunities to team up. I will post the Season 5 Sign-up Post sometime tomorrow. **ONLY PLAYERS WHO SIGN UP BY REPLYING TO THAT POST WILL BE ALLOWED TO PARTICIPATE.** Unlike previous seasons, I will allow for a few days of sign up time instead of only a 24 hour window. This will help allow any interested steemians time to join in. To help make sure that people do not miss it, I will request that anyone who wants to help out wait a day or two and then resteem that sign up post. This will help with visibility. In the previous seasons, I have done almost all the tracking of player choices and inventory documentation manually. This means that I go through the previous post, check *every* reply, copy the usernames into groups, and then post that information in the comment section. Some days, this takes hours of work for me to finish, especially early on in the season when there are so many players. Again, I do not want to restrict the number of players who can join in, so I am going to put more responsibility on the players this season. In Season 5, each player with have to keep track of their own inventory, at least at first. If your player finds a pistol with two rounds of ammunition and a firestarter, then your replies should look something like this: > I choose Option 2 today. Since individual players will be keeping track of their own moves and inventory, the potential for players to be dishonest will arise. A player who makes a decision that gets them nothing in a round may be tempted to claim that they found a pistol. Please do not lie to me. Each decision will be documented on the blockchain, so you will be found out. Also, I will ban you from participation in any of my contest for a time. I do believe in forgiveness, so I will not permanently ban you, but you will have to sit out for a few seasons. I wish that I did not have to say things like this, but since I am giving more responsibility to the players, I felt that I should cover my bases. Please do not let your greed ruin my generosity. ***Also, I've teamed up with @liberty-minded to provide something special and new for Season 5.
