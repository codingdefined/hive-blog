STEEM-POCALYPSE SURVIVAL GAME SEASON 4 RECAP + GAME OVERVIEW & NEW RULES FOR SEASON 5

July 10, 2017

http://i.imgur.com/fS6c6wr.jpg ##

This has been an incredible adventure!
--------- Creating, running, and improving the STEEM-Pocalypse Survival Game Contest has been amazing. First of all, I think that it gives some users a great reason to check in here on steemit *every* day. But even more than that, it inspires some users to interact in a way that they normally may not, which not only builds new friendships, but the community as well. Often in this game steemians who were previously strangers get thrown together into scenarios where they must work together in order to survive, and they have a lot of fun doing it. Of course the opportunity to win hundreds of dollars worth of SBD also has its own appeal. https://img1.steemit.com/0x0/http://i.imgur.com/uVNBbyL.jpg ##
SEASON 4 RECAP
---------- Each season has its own special place in my life. For Season 4, it was a great opportunity for the largest participation so far. There is no way that I want to limit or restrict participation, so I will do my best to always keep this contest open to all. Because of that, I am presuming that more and more steemians will participate in each new season, so Season 5 may have *hundreds* of people signing up initially. Since it is an elimination game, most will eventually have to die, but at least whoever wants to join in can at least give themselves the opportunity to win. In Season 4, I implemented a *solo play* to begin with. Individual steemians had to consistently check in on a daily basis to prove their availability and commitment. This was to help streamline the gameplay and make sure that people playing were actually going to be playing the game. I know that situations arise where not everyone can be available each day, but to move the gameplay forward I need participants to be committed to check in and make decisions once every 24 hour period. Another change for Season 4 is that during the beginning solo play, players got the opportunity to collect resources which would come in handy later. Then, when some of them teamed up, they had more to work with. I think that this added an interesting variable to the gameplay and worked well. https://steemitimages.com/0x0/http://i.imgur.com/YUOX7hw.png As will potentially happen with more participants, Season 4 lasted the longest of any season so far too. Since the prize pool increases on a daily basis, this meant the largest prize pool so far too! The winner, @jznsamuel, took home 825 SBD out of the total prize pool of 1100 SBD. It is crazy to think that someone could make so much money just by playing a game for a few minutes online each day, but that is the opportunity that the STEEM-Pocalypse Survival Game Contest can offer now because of steemit! https://img1.steemit.com/0x0/http://i.imgur.com/uVNBbyL.jpg ##
GAME OVERVIEW
---------- For those of you who are new to this game or unfamiliar with how it works. here is a simple overview of the general concept to help you understand. Basically, this game is a "choose your own adventure" style story game. In each round, I come up with different scenarios in which the players must choose an option. Each option has a predetermined outcome, and some will eventually lead to death. As players die, the remaining ones continue playing until the last players die or only one player/team is left alive. Different variable and scenarios come up each round, some of which are based on reality and are more educational and others are just random scenarios that I come up with. By using their knowledge, researching skills, or maybe just guessing the players attempt to pass each round. Situations like avoiding starvation and staying hydrated are mixed in with various perils and dangerous scenarios. I try to keep it fun, yet challenging. Each player has the responsibility to reply to each new post within 24 hours with their decision based on the options given. Simply put, if you are capable of accessing your steemit account, reading my latest STEEM-Pocalypse post, and replying with the number of the option that you are choosing, you will be able to play. https://img1.steemit.com/0x0/http://i.imgur.com/uVNBbyL.jpg ##

SEASON 5 RULES
---------- In many ways, Season 5 will run a lot like Season 4. Individual players will have to make it past the beginning days in order to proceed. Players who do not reply within the 24 hour response period for each round will be eliminated. Players who make it past the initial rounds will be provided with opportunities to team up. I will post the Season 5 Sign-up Post sometime tomorrow. **ONLY PLAYERS WHO SIGN UP BY REPLYING TO THAT POST WILL BE ALLOWED TO PARTICIPATE.** Unlike previous seasons, I will allow for a few days of sign up time instead of only a 24 hour window. This will help allow any interested steemians time to join in. To help make sure that people do not miss it, I will request that anyone who wants to help out wait a day or two and then resteem that sign up post. This will help with visibility. In the previous seasons, I have done almost all the tracking of player choices and inventory documentation manually. This means that I go through the previous post, check *every* reply, copy the usernames into groups, and then post that information in the comment section. Some days, this takes hours of work for me to finish, especially early on in the season when there are so many players. Again, I do not want to restrict the number of players who can join in, so I am going to put more responsibility on the players this season. In Season 5, each player with have to keep track of their own inventory, at least at first. If your player finds a pistol with two rounds of ammunition and a firestarter, then your replies should look something like this: > I choose Option 2 today. I have a pistol with two bullets and a firestarter. ---------- It will be a lot easier for me to be able to focus on running the game if I do not have to keep track of every item that hundreds of players have every choice that they make. Also, when you have the chance to win hundreds of dollars worth of SBD, I do not think that it is too much for me to ask of you. Thank you in advance for your help. Since individual players will be keeping track of their own moves and inventory, the potential for players to be dishonest will arise. A player who makes a decision that gets them nothing in a round may be tempted to claim that they found a pistol. Please do not lie to me. Each decision will be documented on the blockchain, so you will be found out. Also, I will ban you from participation in any of my contest for a time. I do believe in forgiveness, so I will not permanently ban you, but you will have to sit out for a few seasons. I wish that I did not have to say things like this, but since I am giving more responsibility to the players, I felt that I should cover my bases. Please do not let your greed ruin my generosity. ***Also, I've teamed up with @liberty-minded to provide something special and new for Season 5. The Tallest Moving Observation Tower in the World / 世界上最高的移动观光塔

July 09, 2017

I have no idea how this happened but the weather is the UK has transformed into a tropical heatwave! Maybe I managed to bring a slice of it back home with me when leaving *Crete*. Who knows? Lol. My parents are visiting for a few weeks and they were actually staying home in London while I was on holiday. Now I'm back and welcomed by this "roasting humidity", I thought why not take everyone on a trip to the seaside. The closest one to me being *Brighton* beach! Another reason for visiting there was to check out the *British Airways i360*. **The tallest moving observation tower in the world!** I am so glad I did as the views of *Brighton* from above were incredible and breath-taking. Clear blue skies with not a cloud in sight, the calm sea water below and picturesque green, rolling hills in the distance. **Awe-inspiring stuff, I tell you!** ![2017-07-08 15.10.24.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmUoddB1mmuFa4SUjGD8efJzk2tSTwzmNXxoxj2F4Qjz1P/2017-07-08%2015.10.24.jpg) Located at the end of the West Pier, the *British Airways i360* is a colossal 162 meters tall situated next to the seafront of Brighton. It was designed by architectural company *Marks Barfiedl*, who also created the London Eye. To see it before your very eyes allows you to realise just how much planning and effort must have gone into creating this grand structure. ![2017-07-08 15.13.23.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmUZCVvgY8Ab87A8LRU6JyW2smjhGkPrR7jBFmmRCuVfcZ/2017-07-08%2015.13.23.jpg) ![2017-07-08 15.01.20.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmPzBuCnqhnMTvvcZAJUqoqPqzGzz5GYbTpE27qtgPJkwV/2017-07-08%2015.01.20.jpg) ![2017-07-08 15.01.38.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmSC7uzgwMve25jiF4si1ospM4k44d25PED6rAr7qsLAdr/2017-07-08%2015.01.38.jpg) ![2017-07-08 15.50.27.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmQR9fxLmaSL2ptjK1UTDPukZqok7dj2Zmp7zmJQTFz5WX/2017-07-08%2015.50.27.jpg) This attraction is a relatively new addition to Brighton's entertainment venues with the tower opened only in August last year. It has regular “flight” times every **30 minutes**. The ticket prices are £16 per adult *(£14.50 if booked online)*. The whole operation is run by *British Airways*. The staff were all sporting the official attire of stewards and stewardess's who all displayed their usual helpful and friendly manner. From the entrance onward, you are treated as if boarding a real airliner. I had to go through the routine checking procedures such as bag searches and a full body scan. I suppose is all adds to the authentic experience they were trying to provide us with. ![2017-07-08 15.48.19.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmZsNLYEr9Pb8z6VcdB9danyynXwSXgtMuiA3qhQYxgwn7/2017-07-08%2015.48.19.jpg) Next up was the “departure lounge”. A small cordoned off section comprising of a ringside view of the tower itself and a few deck chairs facing the seafront for more serene relaxation. ![2017-07-08 15.23.16.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmRUcF9pK5HQHrJEbVm5YZhyrfhyChx8vRHLNb3jTAJHP7/2017-07-08%2015.23.16.jpg) Once in the pod, the glass fibre doors shut and we began ascending. The movement was so smooth, it was hardly noticeable at first, lol. Thank god for that as I've always been a little scared of the heights. But this was just perfect. :) ![2017-07-08 15.23.39.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmfPMjbdNVNLBgaaYvsTeC7zF2Rh2nTJHZ2x7vd63t3HZQ/2017-07-08%2015.23.39.jpg) ![2017-07-08 15.54.12.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmRa4EmtMsguRa4NMxc8EFssYBUCiTw4fe24MRSBv8tacQ/2017-07-08%2015.54.12.jpg) ![2017-07-08 15.12.17.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmNw7sbxXsqr76XJ9FZCPE8Jw43cSEYx4Nt8KcxQdzCEaQ/2017-07-08%2015.12.17.jpg) The viewing area was very impressive indeed, with the pod protecting us all in a strong steel and glass encasing. There was plenty of room to walk around and take in the **360** degree spectacle showcasing *all* of Brighton. I was told that on the clearest of days, you could see all the way across to the Isle of Wight in the west! I looked out of it but got too distracted with the other unbelievable sights all around me, lol! ![2017-07-08 15.43.03.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmUU5vax6t6hmzxsat7hH9MuG2YF3oiNBTj6M8YeCrqkyd/2017-07-08%2015.43.03.jpg) ![2017-07-08 15.44.34.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmXwWp2WcR2m6P2EriGdnEVqTeLC3W3zbjeSNb7NN2vxfU/2017-07-08%2015.44.34.jpg) ![2017-07-08 15.43.53.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmWVK1Q8RnxY7y8LEq7Qc9DdV2bgVDTftX1QHJmScDNW4d/2017-07-08%2015.43.53.jpg) ![2017-07-09 01.21.50.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmSKkebUNE883gAQjJq2wrSrWz5w45mhDp8fFrbk8oQkzK/2017-07-09%2001.21.50.jpg) ![2017-07-08 15.41.23.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmPPkdmmj4pbJ26xfskJHCmHPRHZZLiVu53tcXyrDtPAJS/2017-07-08%2015.41.23.jpg) ![2017-07-08 15.40.42.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmdSP6L6N9PqJAwy1VQf6PknL63yYXoL64uz1k2cBBW9tX/2017-07-08%2015.40.42.jpg) Comfortable seating arrangements were provided around the central core for those who cannot bear to look down, lol. Glad I wasn't one of them and bravery managed to get the best of me. :) ![2017-07-08 15.42.18.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmUEU2mvbuvYDEfY33SQtneoYPJVZanqhax47Z9jAaeKZn/2017-07-08%2015.42.18.jpg) On board, there was also the *Nyetimber Sky Bar* serving a range of fine drinks for all the passengers. With English “champagne” at £10 a glass, it was a little expensive I feel. But that's the image they were trying to portray. *A life of luxury and freedom, high up in the clouds above.* ![2017-07-09 01.11.08.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmUPasZeNU18KzCt5mfiQ12Bcq83cVzW2AMyiDzwZ3vrGB/2017-07-09%2001.11.08.jpg) **I can't express how truly stunning the view from the top was!** I've been to Brighton quite a number of times in the past but never thought I'd ever view it in such glorious detail. The *South Coast* being a particularly beautiful sight! However, as you can see, my photographic shots were somewhat hampered by the reflective glass panelling. **I do apologise for that but there didn't seem to be a way round it.** :( The whole process was ultra slick and expertly delivered. My only mild suggestion would be to say that it could have been more interactive. They did have television screens advertising the different countries *British Airways* travel to in the pod, but I thought it would have been nice to have some featured points of interest so people could look out for them. The “flight” was over after twenty-five minutes. The pod came back down *(several minutes duration in each direction)* and the passengers exited via another glass door into a small exhibition centre and souvenir shop. The exhibition boosted photographs and historical videos explaining how the *British Airways i360* was built, which I found very interesting. Not only did it take 200 tonnes of reinforced steel bars to make it's solid foundation, but this tower was also assembled in a very innovative way – **from the top down!** **What a nice little touch to end with!** ![2017-07-08 15.46.11.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmcoRGJ7yjjiASdUeVUVgL3RGqCy9TZm21tV9KNXXpFyFF/2017-07-08%2015.46.11.jpg) ![2017-07-08 15.48.36.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmd9i9B9No81uSQdkTadiPLvm4GveQFsD4rRyJVzu4UCCD/2017-07-08%2015.48.36.jpg) ![2017-07-08 15.47.59.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmZZYLvqciyBjtVVAvHZTwZSKvoAqZ6cjQbrez1WxmnVJx/2017-07-08%2015.47.59.jpg) The ride was memorable and the views utterly fantastic and not for the faint of heart. An overall fantastic experience that I would recommend to anyone visiting good old *Brighton* seaside! ![2017-07-09 01.15.19.jpg](https://steemitimages.com/DQmaXhNKd5WuydmRvT9TAwJvwjnnFqvh1bBfZto6y4xfLj3/2017-07-09%2001.15.19.jpg)

这阵子英国的天气异常炎热，或许是我把希腊岛的热天气给带回家了，哈哈。 父母正好来伦敦探望我几周，我决定带他们到海边玩玩。离伦敦最近的海滩就是布莱顿海滩，我们也顺便参观一下这里的新景点“英航i360 ”- 世界上最高的移动观光塔！ 事实证明这个决定是正确的，观光塔上的风景美得让人难以置信，头上蓝天白云，脚下平静蔚蓝的海水，密密麻麻的城市建筑，还有远处绵延起伏的山丘，真是风景如画! 英航i360位于布莱顿西码头的末端，距离布莱顿海滨仅有一公里。观光塔高162米，是由建造伦敦眼的同家建筑公司Marks Barfiedl设计的。亲眼看到这么壮观的建筑，我也可以想象得出多少规划和努力才能创造出这么宏伟的结构！ 塔楼仅在去年8月才开放，算是布莱顿的一个新景点。每30分钟一次“航班”，成人票价为16英镑（如果在线预订，则为14.50英镑）。 整个观光塔都由英国航空公司运作，包括穿着正式服装的空姐以及他们彬彬有礼的服务态度。从入口开始，我就被视为登上真正客机的乘客，必须通过例行检查程序，包含随身物品搜查及全身扫描。我想这些额外的细节都为大家提供了更真实的乘机体验，也增添了不少乐趣，呵呵。 接下来我进入了“出发休息室”。一个小小的平台，就在观光塔的入口旁边。这儿提供了不少躺椅供大家在静待“航班 ”出发的同时，休闲欣赏海边美景！ 进入观光塔后，玻璃门关上，我们就开始缓慢上升了。整个过程还挺顺利，我几乎感觉不到它的移动，真是谢天谢地，因为我还是有点儿畏高的，呵呵。 在塔上观光的确令人印象深刻。观光塔外型有如甜甜圈，整个外壳是由坚硬的钢化玻璃制作，非常安全。塔里面有充足的走动空间，大家可以360度地自由观赏布莱顿的全景，最远的甚至能看到西部的怀特岛呢！ 塔中央的位置还为有需要的人提供了舒适的座位。 这里面有一个名叫Nyetimber Sky的酒吧，为乘客提供一系列精致的饮品。英国的香槟酒10英镑一杯，我感觉有点儿贵。这也许是英航试图为大家描绘的他们的品牌形象：高高在上穿梭于云层中，提供豪华自由的生活方式。 我以前来过布莱顿几次，但从未如此清晰细致地俯视它。南海岸的美丽景色，简直是言语都难以表达的美！可是正如你们所见，我拍的不少照片都因为反光玻璃导致部分不清晰，在此抱歉啦！ 整个“飞行”过程非常顺利，我唯一的建议是增加互动性。塔上的电视屏幕只是宣传乘坐英航到不同国家旅游，我想如果能在屏幕上展示布莱顿不同方位的景点，提醒初到布莱顿游客注意观看，那就更完美了。 二十五分钟后，整个“飞行”过程结束了，乘客通过另一个玻璃门退出到一个小型展览厅和纪念品商店。 展览厅里的照片和视频，为大家介绍了英航i360观光塔是如何建造的。原来它不仅需要200吨强化钢筋以作为其坚实的基础，而且这座塔也是以非常创新的“自上往下”的方式组装！我对这些额外的信息特别感兴趣。 这次布莱顿英航观光塔一游很令人难忘，犹如梦幻般的飞行体验，风景更是美丽如画！强烈推荐给所有来布莱顿观光的朋友们，呵呵。
