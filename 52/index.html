    Are you Vegetarian?

    August 24, 2017

    ### I would like to ask you a question "Are you Vegetarian?". ## I once had a conversation with my friend on being vegetarian. He asked me Are you Vegetarian?, I said Yes. Then he said that he is also a Vegetarian but he eats fish. I said dude seriously you eat fish and you are saying you are vegetarian. **A Vegetarian is a person who does not eat meat, poultry, egg, and fish** #### Vegetarians are classified in three ways. #### 1. Vegans or total vegetarians who exclude all animal products(meat, poultry, fish, eggs, milk and other dairy products). Many vegans also do not eat honey. 2. Lactovegetarians exclude meat, poultry, fish, and eggs but include dairy products. 3. Lacto-ovo-vegetarians exclude meat, poultry, and fish but include dairy products. and eggs. Now the funny part, "How a friend will introduce you to his friends if you are a vegetarian?". Hey, all this is my friend and he is a vegetarian. Seriously this is the introduction! Now coming to the health benefit of being vegetarian. Do you know Brad Pitt, Steven Spielberg and many are becoming vegetarian due to the health concerns? According to American Diabetic Association vegetarians are at lower risk for developing Heart disease, diabetes, obesity, and hypertension. There are a number of reasons for becoming vegetarian. Some people are vegetarian because they are following some religion. Some people are vegetarian because they are against animal cruelty. Some believe that being vegetarian is overall a healthy diet to follow. How many times have you asked a vegetarian why they are vegetarian and end up having an argument with them. The most common question to vegetarians is, what you eat since you don't have enough varieties of food. According to a great scientist Einstein: "Nothing will benefit human health and increase the chances for survival of life on Earth as much as the evolution to a vegetarian diet." And I can proudly say I am a Vegeterian.

    Maharajas' Express in India

    August 23, 2017

    In my last post, I have discussed about one of the luxurious trains in India [Palace on Wheels](https://steemit.com/luxury/@codingdefined/palace-on-wheels-in-india). Bdw in India we do have a lot of luxurious trains and one of them is Maharajas's Express. Maharajas' Express, is an initiative by IRCTC (Indian Railways Catering and Tourism Corporation), is equivalent to the Orient Express of Europe. https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/b/be/Maharajas%27_Express.jpg By Aswin Krishna Poyil (Own work) [CC0], via Wikimedia Commons It consists of - **Mayur Mahal** The Sitting Lounge of the Maharajas' Express is the perfect place to forget your tiredness of the day’s sightseeing. Just sit back, relax and admire the comfortable ambiance. **Rang Mahal** This is a restaurant with its serene interiors and glittering furnishing also houses 42 guests at a time and serves an eclectic melange of world cuisines. **Safari Bar** The lounge bar housed within Maharaja Express that guests can visit in order to unwind in a perfectly nonchalant yet suave ambiance after a tiring day of sightseeing and travel. **Routes** There are mainly 7 routes which Maharajas' take and the they have different name 1. The Indian Splendor (Delhi - Agra - Ranthambore - Jaipur - Bikaner - Jodhpur - Udaipur - Balasinor - Mumbai) 2. The Indian Panorama (Delhi - Jaipur - Ranthambore - Fatehpur Sikri - Agra - Gwalior - Khajuraho - Varanasi - Lucknow - Delhi) 3. Treasures of India (Delhi - Agra - Ranthambore - Jaipur - Delhi) 4. Gems of India (Delhi - Agra - Ranthambore - Jaipur - Delhi) 5. The Heritage of India (Mumbai - Ajanta - Udaipur - Jodhpur - Bikaner - Jaipur - Ranthambore - Agra - Delhi) 6. The Southern Jewels (Trivandrum - Chettinad - Mahabalipuram - Mysore - Hampi - Goa - Mumbai) 7. The Southern Sojourn (Mumbai - Goa - Hampi - Mysore - Cochin - Kumarakom - Trivandrum) Maharajas' Express can take up-to 84 passengers and have different cabins like Deluxe Cabin ( Area: 112 Sq. Ft ), Junior Suite ( Area: 150 Sq. Ft ), Suite ( Area : 220 Sq. Ft ) and Presidential Suite ( Area: 448 Sq. Ft ). It also has Dining Car, Bar and Lounge to improve your expreience. **Fares** **Heritage of India (8 Days/ 7 Nights)** Deluxe Cabins - USD 6,840.00 Junior Suite - USD 9,890.00 Suite - USD 13,800.00 Presidential Suite - USD 23,700.00 **Treasures of India (4 Days/ 3 Nights)** Deluxe Cabins - USD 3,850.00 Junior Suite - USD 4,950.00 Suite - USD 7,600.00 Presidential Suite - USD 12,900.00 **Gems of India(4 Days/ 3 Nights)** Deluxe Cabins - USD 3,850.00 Junior Suite - USD 4,950.00 Suite - USD 7,600.00 Presidential Suite - USD 12,900.00 **Indian Panorama (8 Days/ 7 Nights)** Deluxe Cabins - USD 5,980.00 Junior Suite - USD 9,460.00 Suite - USD 13,800.00 Presidential Suite - USD 23,700.00 **Indian Splendor (8 Days/ 7 Nights)** Deluxe Cabins - USD 5,980.00 Junior Suite - USD 9,460.00 Suite - USD 13,800.00 Presidential Suite - USD 23,700.00 **The Southern Jewels (8 Days/ 7 Nights)** Deluxe Cabins - USD 7,530.00 Junior Suite - USD 10,880.00 Suite - USD 15,180.00 Presidential Suite - USD 26,070.00 **The Southern Sojourn (8 Days/ 7 Nights)** Deluxe Cabins - USD 7,530.00 Junior Suite - USD 10,880.00 Suite - USD 15,180.00 Presidential Suite - USD 26,070.00 So, next time on your travel to India, do consider enjoying this awesome luxurious train
